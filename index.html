Hi, I'm Tina.

We live in Wednesbury in our family home. I'm a loving Mum to Connor, born 2009 and Holly, born 2012, who both attend Wilkinson's Primary School in Bilston. We also live with my Husband and children's Father, Dave.

I have been child minding in a professional capacity since 2010, but I have spent most of my years looking after children in various forms, starting from age 14 as a baby sitter.

I am fully qualified for my role, having completed much more than the basic training required by law. I am fully trained in first aid, food hygiene, child safety, and much more. All original training certificates are available in my setting for you to view. I am also fully registered and approved with OFSTED.

Previous to this career, I was a retail store manager for over 7 years and the many skills I developed then - team management, customer service, conflict resolution, and more - I find continue to still be useful today.

I offer a number of services, details of which you can find below.

Child Minding

I have the experience and facilities to take children of any age, from 3 month old babies through to children of primary school age. I work between the hours of 7am and 6pm and I understand the need to be flexible.

Both my Husband and I have been fully DBS checked, and these are available for you to view at any time. The safety of the children in my care is always my number one priority.

School Runs

I provide a reliable school drop off and pick up service for Wilkinson's Primary School in Bilston. I have a 7 seater vehicle so there is usually plenty of room.

You would need to have your child with me no later than 8.15am for drop off, and I usually arrive back around 4pm for collection.

Healthy Meals

I provide breakfast and lunches for all children in my care during meal times. All meals are healthy and balanced with the occasional snack throughout the day too!

I fully comply with the Food Information Regulations introduced in 2014 and can accommodate all of your child's nutritional and dietary needs.
